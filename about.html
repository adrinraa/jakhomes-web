About Us

Property and Interior Design

Established Track Record For Designing and Delivering outstanding Properties in Jakarta's Prime Location Since 2008. Jakarta Homes is a market leader within Jakarta's High End Prime residential Market. Our success and foundation is based upon our meticulous understanding of design and space planning alongside our defined bespoke model to execute and deliver an exceptional end product. Our in house design team prides itself on understated and timeless design, not driven by a particular style but compliments its context which in turn creates unique properties for our developments and our private clients each time

The Journey of Jakarta Homes

Since founding Jakarta Homes in 2008, Dian has consistently showcased an exceptional eye for detail and an in-depth understanding of the critical role space planning plays in successful property development. Her approach goes beyond simply designing spaces; she meticulously ensures that every project not only meets the specific needs and preferences of buyers but also aligns seamlessly with the demographic and character of the surrounding area. Through her dedication and expertise, Dian has managed to develop a wide range of high-end residential properties, many of which have achieved outstanding success, setting record prices and establishing a benchmark for quality in the industry.

Indra Gunawan
